☕ Retail Performance Analysis of a Multi-Location Coffee Shop Chain

🧭 Project Overview

This project analyzes transactional sales data from a multi-location coffee shop business to uncover revenue drivers, customer purchasing patterns, and operational insights across stores and time periods.

The analysis was conducted using Excel, including Power Query for data cleaning, Pivot Tables for modeling, and a dashboard for visualization.

⸻

🎯 Business Objectives
	•	Identify primary revenue drivers
	•	Compare performance across store locations
	•	Analyze peak demand periods
	•	Evaluate product category contributions
	•	Provide actionable business recommendations

⸻

📊 Key Insights
	•	Coffee products generate the majority of revenue, supported by tea and bakery items
	•	Hell’s Kitchen store outperforms other locations significantly
	•	Peak sales occur during morning hours (8 AM — 10 AM), driven by commuter demand
	•	Average basket size is low, indicating strong opportunities for upselling
	•	Sales patterns remain relatively stable across weekdays

⸻

💡 Business Recommendations
	•	Introduce bundled offers to increase basket size
	•	Optimize staffing during peak hours
	•	Investigate drivers of underperforming locations
	•	Improve promotion and placement of low-performing categories

	•	Pivot Tables
